Scope and Overview

We at FTX Trading Ltd. and the FTX Debtors (collectively, "FTX", "we", "us", or "our") describe in this Privacy Notice how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process your personal data in connection with the AML/KYC Customer Claims Portal. This Privacy Notice applies to all personal data collected through the portal.

Collection and Purpose of Processing Personal Data

In connection with the processing and monitoring of claims and distributions, FTX collects various categories of personal data. We collect this data to verify your identity, process your claim, prevent fraud and money laundering, and comply with legal obligations.

A. Information You Provide to Us Directly

We may collect the following personal data that you provide to us:

  • Identification Information (First and last name, date and place of birth, gender, phone number, email address, physical address, government identification number, etc.)
  • Financial Information (Bank account information, transaction history, wallet addresses)
  • Employment Information (Job title, office location, source of income)
  • Tax Information (Information provided in connection with the completion of tax forms)

B. Information Collected Automatically (Use of Cookies)

We may collect personal data automatically when you use our Portal. This includes usage data, device information (IP address, device type, browser type), and information collected through cookies and similar technologies.

C. Information Collected from Other Sources

We may obtain information about you from third-party sources, such as public databases, identity verification partners, and blockchain analytics providers.

Collection and Purpose of Processing Sensitive Personal Data, Including Biometric Data

In certain circumstances, we may collect sensitive personal data, including biometric data (e.g., facial scan for identity verification). We process this data strictly for identity verification and fraud prevention purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

FTX processes personal data to comply with various legal and regulatory obligations (e.g., AML/KYC requirements). We also process data based on our legitimate interests in managing the claims process and preventing fraud.

Personal Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with third-party service providers, legal advisors, and relevant authorities as required by law or to facilitate the claims process.

International Transfers

Personal data collected through the Portal may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your data during such transfers.

Data Security

FTX implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Children's Information

The Portal is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Links to Other Websites

The Portal may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

Individual Rights and How to Exercise Them

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

  • To request access to personal data
  • To request correction of personal data
  • To request deletion of personal data
  • To object to processing

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – European Representative

Pursuant to Article 27 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), FTX has appointed a representative in the EU. You can contact them regarding matters pertaining to the GDPR.

UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – UK Representative

FTX has appointed a representative in the UK for data protection matters.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. This section describes your CCPA rights and how to exercise them.

Consequences of Failure to Provide Personal Data

Where FTX requires personal data to comply with legal obligations, failure to provide such data may result in our inability to process your claim.

Changes to the Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this notice.
